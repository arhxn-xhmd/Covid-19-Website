How Virus Spreads

How Contagion Coronavirus

Coronavirus spreads easily through air and close contact. When an infected person talks, coughs, or sneezes, tiny droplets spread the virus quickly. It also spreads by touching contaminated surfaces and then touching the face or mouth.

Corona Virus Symptoms

Coronavirus symptoms generally begin with mild signs like fever, cough, and tiredness. Some people may also experience loss of taste or smell, sore throat, or difficulty breathing.

Prevention Coronavirus

Prevention of coronavirus involves following healthy and responsible habits to stay safe and protect others. Washing hands regularly, wearing masks, keeping social distance, and avoiding crowded places greatly reduce the chances of infection and help control the spread of the virus.
